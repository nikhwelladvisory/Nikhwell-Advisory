About us

What we
work on?

At Nikhwell Advisory, we specialize in delivering data-driven financial solutions that empower businesses to make smarter decisions. Our expertise spans Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A), Budgeting and Forecasting, Financial Reporting, and Data Visualization. With a strong focus on accuracy, transparency, and performance, we help organizations transform financial data into actionable insights. Leveraging tools like Excel, Power BI, and SQL, we enable clients to optimize operations across Mutual Funds, Insurance, and Stock Market domains — driving sustainable growth and measurable success.

With a commitment to innovation and precision, Nikhwell Advisory bridges the gap between data and decision-making. Our approach combines financial expertise with analytical insights to provide customized solutions that align with each client’s goals. We believe in building long-term partnerships by delivering measurable results, improving business performance, and ensuring financial clarity at every level. Whether it’s streamlining reporting, enhancing forecasting accuracy, or visualizing performance metrics, we help businesses turn financial data into a powerful tool for growth.
